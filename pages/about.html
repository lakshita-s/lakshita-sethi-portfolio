About Lakshita Sethi

Parsons · BFA Design & Technology • NYC · Dubai • Interactive Media · Creative Coding

Lakshita Sethi is a driven creative professional with experience in multidisciplinary art, design, and writing. With a background shaped by a multicultural upbringing, and a passion for problem-solving, she has a creative and analytical approach to her work.

Interested in interactive experiences, she admires emotional, empathetic and highly intentional design. She likes to bring these values in her work, and also uses philosophical themes in her artistic expression; generally creating from a place of personal experience, or an evaluation of the human condition.

Fueled by a desire to create meaningful connections- through interactive media, consequential narratives, or strategic solutions, and more. Lakshita is particularly interested in developing skills in human-centered design, user-focused creativity, and systemic education reform, to contribute to projects that prioritize innovation and impact. Ultimately, her goal is to work at the intersection of design, technology, and humanity, to build a better, healed future.

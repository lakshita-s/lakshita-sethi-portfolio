Eleven and a Half

2026 · HTML/CSS · Astro · Literary journal's digital archive

Eleven and a Half is the student-run literary magazine of Eugene Lang College at The New School. I created this site to act as a digital archive of it's past, present, and future issues. I fully digitized the latest issue, Issue 14, and continue the process of digitizing each previous issue as well. In the meantime, they can still be read via attached PDFs.

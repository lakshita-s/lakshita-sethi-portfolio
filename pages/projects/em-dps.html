Deconstructing "Dead Poets Society"

2024 · Deconstructed Video · Adobe Premier Pro · Duration: 4:13

This edit reinterprets the final scenes of Dead Poets Society, centering on Neil Perry’s death to foreground the societal and familial pressures that lead to his suicide. Drawing from Martin Arnold’s experimental techniques, the edit uses repetition, rewinding, inversion, and hyper-saturation to intensify emotional tension. Set to A Quick One Before the Eternal Worm Devours Connecticut by Have a Nice Life, the piece reinforces Neil’s death as the result of sustained pressure and expectation.

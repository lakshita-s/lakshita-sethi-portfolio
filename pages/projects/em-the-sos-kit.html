The S.O.S. Kit

2024 · Zine · Civic design

A proposed additional part to the traditional emergency to-go bag, to aid the mental effects of crisis situations. The S.O.S. ‘Save our Souls’ Kit, does not replace critical survival supplies, but instead offers emotional, psychological, and cultural comfort, providing mental support. The standard supplies provide protection, whilst this kit protects the soul and the spirit. Suggested categories of items to add to your kit include comfort, expression, grounding, creativity, and connection to land. Reach brochure for further detail.

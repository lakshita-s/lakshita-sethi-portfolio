The Craft Stock

2026 · HTML/CSS/JS · Tool

The Craft Stock is a peer-to-peer art/design supplies and materials exchange for New School students. Inspired by the materials exchange run by the school, I wanted to make a 24 hours, location-based digital materials exchange, so students at the New School have easy access to materials. New York is a huge city, and being an art/design student can be expensive and wasteful. My aim is to help commuters, students with smaller budgets still achieve great aspirations for projects, reduce waste, and foster easy connection and the wondrous act of sharing within our community.

A scene study inspired by the landscape of a tea plantation in Munnar, India. Rolling hills layered with rows of tea plants are broken up by narrow dirt paths, with silver oak trees and distant mountains framing the scene. The project focuses on slow navigation through space, treating the landscape less as a destination and more as something to wander through and observe.

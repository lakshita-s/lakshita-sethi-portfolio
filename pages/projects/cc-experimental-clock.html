Experimental Clock

September 2025 · p5.js

Hover over the clock to reveal genres.

View Live Sketch ↗ View Code ↗

This clock visualizes time through music rather than numbers. Designed as a vinyl record, each hour is assigned a musical genre based on its emotional and psychological impact. The central imagery of the vinyl shifts with each genre of the hour.


The project explores how music functions as a personal and psychological timekeeper- shaping mood, energy, and perception throughout the day; reimagining time as something felt and experienced, rather than measured.

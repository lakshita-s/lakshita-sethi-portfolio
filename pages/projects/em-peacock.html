Peacock

2024 · Wearable Sculpture · Wire

Inspired by the peacock- a symbol of beauty, pride, and my cultural heritage- this piece explores identity, self-expression, and transformation. The intricate wirework mimics the organic flow of peacock feathers, depicting personal memories and connections to the magnificent animal.

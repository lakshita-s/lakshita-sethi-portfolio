Sanskrit Jigsaw Puzzle

2024 · Product Design· Wood (laser-cut and engraved)
· Size: 8” x 8”

Process

Sanskrit is the ancestral language of Hindi, using the same alphabet. This piece comes from an investigation into the typography of this alphabet. Taking from the basic segments, edges, and curves present in the letters, I designed a jigsaw puzzle; each of the connections contains a shape present in a letter. I used Adobe Illustrator to add a layer of a Sanskrit folktale. Cut using a laser-cutter.

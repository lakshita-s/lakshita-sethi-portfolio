Data Portrait "Rings"

2025 · p5.js · Motion

Press space to begin animation.

View Live Sketch ↗ View Code ↗

A personal data portrait tracking how often I put on and remove my rings over the course of a week. Each bell represents one ring, with sound and color changes marking moments of wear and removal—green for on, red for off. The hanging bell structure is inspired by traditional Kutch bell decor from India.

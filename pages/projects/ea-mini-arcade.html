Mini Arcade Lamp

2025 · Arduino · First EA Project

A mini arcade-inspired musical lamp that combines interactive electronics with everyday functionality. The goal was to design a usable electronic object that integrates sound, color, and physical controls into a single system. Inspired by classic arcade interfaces, and the nostalgia of specific favorite childhood games, the lamp uses buttons and a joystick to control lighting and audio, turning a familiar form into an interactive lighting piece.

Creative Coding

I am worth 192 points

2026 · p5.js · Interactive · Commentary

Click on the points for a deeper anaylsis; dissect your flaws, find your "beauty score".

View Live Sketch ↗ View Code ↗

An experimental camera made using ml5.js models (FaceMesh and BodyPoze-BlazePoze) to make a commentary on the unrealistic beauty standards of today’s society, and the recent rise of “looksmaxxing”. Meant to be used by people who participate in this devastating system, it crudely assigns negative and positive points to facial features, and gives a "beauty score" to the user. Everything is entirely randomized, as the point is not to judge the user, but to judge this facet of society; an ongoing struggle that we all face.
